Spatial Data Analysis of Airbnbs

Here is a map I made using scraped data from Airbnb on the number of listings available in Amsterdam from 2016 to 2020. This map illustrates the density of Airbnbs in a gradient, with overarching hotspots indicating neighborhoods with a statistically significant number of Airbnbs. With the increase of unregulated short-term rentals like Airbnb within major city centers like Amsterdam, many local populations are being displaced through forms of gentrification. A spatial data analysis of these cities, such as this, serves to indicate the prominence of such short-term rentals to provide a means of regulating them within local economies.
